# 🚀 Ohio Ledger: Future Features Roadmap

This document outlines potential enhancements and new modules to evolve Ohio Ledger into a world-class financial management platform.

---

## 💎 Premium Feature Ideas

### 1. 🤖 AI Financial Insights (CFO Mode)
*   **Spend Analysis**: Detect unusual spikes in specific expense categories.
*   **Predictive Cash Flow**: Forecast cash positions for the next 30 days based on historical trends.
*   **Neural Learning**: Improve OCR accuracy by learning from user-corrected merchant/account mappings.

### 2. 🧾 Pro Invoicing & Billing
*   **Custom Invoices**: Generate professional PDF invoices with business branding.
*   **Customer Portal**: Allow customers to view their outstanding balances.
*   **Automated Receivables**: Link invoices directly to the 'Accounts Receivable' ledger for real-time tracking.

### 3. 📦 Inventory & Stock Management [DONE]
*   **Stock Ledger**: Real-time tracking of item quantities and valuation.
*   **COGS Calculator**: Automatic calculation of Cost of Goods Sold using FIFO/Weighted Average methods.
*   **Low Stock Alerts**: Notifications when inventory levels drop below a defined safety threshold.

### 4. 🏦 Bank Statement Reconciliation
*   **CSV/Excel Imports**: Bulk upload bank statements to auto-match transactions.
*   **Auto-Reconciliation Engine**: Link bank rows to existing journal entries based on date and amount.
*   **Missing Entry Detection**: Highlight bank movements that haven't been recorded in the ledger yet.

### 5. 🎯 Budgeting & Performance Tracking
*   **Account Budgets**: Set spending limits for specific expense accounts.
*   **Comparative Reporting**: Visual "Budget vs. Actual" charts.
*   **Threshold Notifications**: Alerts when departments or categories exceed 80% of their allocated budget.

### 6. 🌍 Multi-Currency & Global Accounting
*   **Dual Reporting**: View reports in base currency (NGN) and secondary currency (USD) simultaneously.
*   **Live FX API**: Auto-calculate exchange rate gains/losses during settlement.

### 7. 👥 Team Collaboration & Permissions
*   **User Roles**: Granular access control for Admins, Accountants, and Viewers.
*   **Audit Logs**: Detailed history of who modified which transaction and when.

---

## 🛠 Strategic Priorities
1.  **Phase 1**: Invoicing & Receivables (Drive revenue tracking).
2.  **Phase 2**: Bank Reconciliation (Decrease manual data entry).
3.  **Phase 3**: AI Insights (Add high-value intelligence).
